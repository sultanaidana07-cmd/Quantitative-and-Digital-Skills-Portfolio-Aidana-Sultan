This project demonstrates my quantitative and digital skills in financial data analysis using Excel. I collected historical stock price data from Yahoo Finance and prepared the dataset for analysis by calculating weekly returns. I applied statistical methods including average return, variance, standard deviation, Sharpe ratio, covariance, correlation, and regression analysis. The analysis examined the relationship between the returns of Kaspi.kz, the Freedom 100 Emerging Markets ETF, and the S&P 500 market index. This project highlights my ability to work with financial datasets, perform statistical analysis, interpret investment performance, and present analytical results in a structured and professional format.
